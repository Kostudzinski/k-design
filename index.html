My name is Konrad Studziński and I have been working as a designer and creator of websites, applications, visual identification, vector and bitmap graphics and print materials for the last 5 years.

After completing my studies at the Faculty of Architecture, I finished the Etica Academic Program in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign, and was involved in the preparation of various types of graphic designs and print materials for the order. I also completed a comprehensive Academy 108 course, preparing Front End Developers for HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, SASS, RWD and WordPress. For the last year I have been working on designing and coding pages in WordPress. Getting to know the possibilities of the system and marketing plug-ins led me to completing a course in WordPress and PHP.

I believe that the combination of art and design with advanced technologies gives unlimited possibilities. I'm a fan of everything that is clear and intuitive and makes an impact on a user. Besides designing and coding, I am passionate about UI / UX design and modern web design trends.
